Insider's Book I live in China . . . I can say I know this place pretty well, which is a tough claim to make. If you don't live here, and don't have first hand knowledge, but you've been really following China over the years - you'll enjoy this book. I give it a 5-star, and it deserves this rating . . . but you should have a good understanding of what's been happening with China since Mao died in 1976 -to the present ... in order to fully appreciate this book.  Go For it if you want to know more about the REAL CHINA
