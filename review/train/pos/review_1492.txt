The Missing Piece of the Puzzle The perspectives, sources and discussions this book provides are essential for anyone to understand political life in China and all the myriad ways in which it effects life here. On the surface, nearly all Chinese believe that the CCP is the sole protector of the Chinese nation and its culture and as a result enjoys enormous support at home. Probe a little deeper into individual issues and the gaps in this theory become self evident. This book goes a long way in exposing and explaining the Party's tenuous hold on power.
