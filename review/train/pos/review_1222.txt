An Important History Lesson Hard to put down, painful to read.  American hubris gullibility, and greed drove foreign policy in Asia since the 19th century.  It's all documented here.  If you haven't heard of the "China Lobby" or know who the "China Hands" were, this book will fill you in.  The sad story of American policy failures in the far east.  A big lesson here about the danger of powerful media with an agenda.The opium trade, "Open Door Policy", Japanese invasion of China, Mao Zedong's rise, WWII, Korea, Viet Nam -- Bradley does an excellent job of explaining how it all occurred.
