An excellent, well documented - although biased - history of our delusional attitude towards China from the early 19th century to the present.Bradley presents how we were, as a nation, deceived by our government, who were deceived by peoples totally dedicated to their agenda of a christianized democratic China led by Chian Kai-chek.  A really good read for those interested in the real history of how we let communism rule in the far east and how we were embroiled in Korean and Vietnamese wars.
