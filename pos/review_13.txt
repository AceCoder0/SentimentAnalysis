Captivating Historical Fiction In true Edward Rutherford fashion, China is a well researched, interesting, easy to read, epic novel that informs and brings history to life, while drawing the reader into the lives of  well developed characters—both historical and fictional. Rutherford seamlessly weaves fact and fiction together and captivates the reader’s attention with day-to-day accounts of life in China during the 19th century from the perspective of several characters from various social, economic, political and ethnic groups who interact to reveal a pivotal era in Chinese history. Once you get started it is hard to put it down!
