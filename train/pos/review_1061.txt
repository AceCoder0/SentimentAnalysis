A Mandatory History This incredibly valuable book should be mandatory reading for anyone who is interested in 20th Century history.  Others have explained why in their review submissions.  To flesh out this story, those interested should well consider reading the following:The Imperial Cruise (Bradley), which shows how TR's Manifest Destiny ideas and racist theories set the stage for the events in China and Japan that followed.The Rising Sun (Toland), Far and away the best all around history of the Pacific War, and the events in Japan that led up to it.Stilwell and the American Experience in China (Tuchman), Backs up and adds detail of what Bradley relates in The China Mirage.
